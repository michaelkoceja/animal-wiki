A lynx is a medium sized cat. They are similar size to bobcats. The name Lynx originated in Middle English via Latin from the Greek word λύγξ. Their name references their reflective eyes.

A lovebird is a small parrot. They are very socialble and affectionate. Lovebirds are native to Africa and Madagascar. Their name comes from the parrots' strong, monogamous pair bonding and the long periods of time pairs spend sitting together.

The common hippopotamus, or hippo, is a log, mostly herbivorous mamal in sub-Saharan Africa. Its name comes from ancient Greek for "river horse."
